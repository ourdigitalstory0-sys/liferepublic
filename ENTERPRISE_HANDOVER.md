# ENTERPRISE HANDOVER: Life Republic Digital Asset

The Kolte Patil Life Republic platform has been transformed into a sovereign digital authority for the 390-acre township in Hinjewadi, Pune.

## 1. Core Architecture
- **Framework**: React 19 + Vite (Enterprise Build-chain).
- **Data Layer**: Supabase (JS SDK) with hardened RLS (Row Level Security).
- **Service Layer**: Fully typed `api.ts` with built-in lead scoring and personalization engine.

## 2. Sovereign Search (SEO)
- **Schema v3.2**: Graph-based JSON-LD across all project tiers.
- **Internal Linking**: High-entropy semantic mesh (NRI Hub, Township Intelligence, Investment Analysis).
- **Sitemap**: Automated generation integrated into the build-hook.

## 3. Conversion Intelligence
- **Sovereign Concierge**: Multi-step lead qualification form (Interest -> Budget -> Contact).
- **Smart Contact**: Time-aware floating contact bubble (Highlighting WhatsApp vs Call based on business hours).
- **Personalization**: 'Recently Viewed' projects engine powered by LocalStorage & API.

## 4. Maintenance & Scaling
- **Data Sync**: Use `scripts/generate_sync_sql.ts` for staging-to-prod migrations.
- **Project Updates**: All data is driven by the `projects` table in Supabase; edits reflect instantly via the `api.ts` layer.
- **AI Readiness**: Structured `township_kb.json` is ready for future LLM/RAG integration.
